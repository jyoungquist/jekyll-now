About Me

I am a second year computer science PhD student at the University of Florida, working under Dr. Jorg Peters. I am currently working on a small DARPA TRADES team focused on designing microstructure for 3d printing that is inspired by observations from nature, using tools from a diverse set of mathematics including algebraic geometry, differential geometry, and numerical analysis.

Apart from 3d printing, I have interests in computer graphics, curves and surfaces, algorithms, and CS theory. I also have a laymans interest in philosophy and theology, read as often as I can, and enjoy working out.
